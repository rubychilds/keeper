If you delete this publicly viewable information, copies may remain in archives or other storage logs kept by the Keeper Service. Other site users may have also copied that data (i.e. comments) outside of our control.

(b) Non-Personally Identifiable Information: (If you’re sending it to a third-party) We exercise the option to provide non-personally identifiable information (i.e. URL’s and Data Usage) with interested third parties to help them gauge metrics for particular services and those of our partners.

(c) Instances Where We Are Required to Share Your Information: Keeper will hand over your information where compelled to legally do so. We may also do so if it is likely such action is necessary to abide by the law and law enforcement orders to ensure compliance with the Terms of Service and to protect the security or integrity of our Service. If it is necessary to protect other legal rights we may also do so.

(d) What Happens In The Event Of A Change Of Control: We may buy or sell/divest/transfer the company (including any shares in the company), or any combination of its products, services, assets and/or businesses. If the service changes hands, all data stored will also be transferred into their new management structure. You will be notified of such changes, as well as any consequences it may have for you personal information.

Compromise of Personal Information

If that personal information is compromised by way of a security breach, Keeper will immediately alert our customers both as good practice and in compliance with various data protection laws.

At any time, you reserve the right to request that we correct or erase the personal data or cease the processing of your personal data if you contend it is inaccurate. In addition, you can object to the processing should it violate legal grounds. We must then evaluate and take action based on your lodgement.

Data portability, that is, being able to request data in a desired format, is a further entitlement you have as a customer.

Should you feel necessary, you can also directly file a complaint with the local supervisory authority.

Information Retention

If you have an account with Keeper, we will retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. We will retain and use your information as necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce our agreements

Children’s Privacy

The minimum age to use Keeper is 18.

Ensuring children's privacy is critical. As such, Keeper does not, gather information from anyone under the age of 13.

If children’s data has been unintentionally gathered, this information will be erased immediately.

If you are under the impression, that we may have data on children under 13, please let us know immediately at help@keeper.services
